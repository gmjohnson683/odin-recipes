Steak Recipe

Dish Description

Hello fellow Odinites! Here is a recipe for some delicious sirloin steaks.

These steaks go great with asparagus and mashed potatoes. I hope you all enjoy!

Ingredients & Kitchen Utensils Needed

  • Sirloin steak
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Tongs
  • Cast iron skillet
  • Baking sheet

Cooking Steps

  1. Lay your steak(s) on a baking sheet/plate (depending on your refrigerator size)
  2. Grab a couple of pinches of salt and sprinkle moderately on the front and back sides of the steak(s)
  3. Now with the black pepper, repeat the action followed in the previous step
  4. Grab a pinch of of onion powder and sprinkle lightly on the front and back sides of the steak(s)
  5. Now with the garlic powder, repeat the action followed in the previous step
  6. Leave the seasoned steak(s) in the refrigerator for a minimum of 1 hour (1 day max)
  7. After the allotted time, pull the steak(s) out and pre-heat the oven for 300 F / 149 C
  8. While the oven is pre-heating, prepare the cast iron skillet with a generous amount of olive oil at the highest stove eye setting
  9. Set the steak(s) in the cast iron skillet and allow them to sear for 3-4 minutes on both sides
  10. After searing, stick the cast iron skillet in the oven for 4-10 minutes (depending on how rare you prefer your steak(s))
