Hash Browns Recipe

Dish Description

Hello fellow Odinites! This dish represents my replication of a hash brown order you would get at Waffle House. This recipe allows you to make your very own covered and chunked hashbrowns!

If you want some additional food, this dish pairs well with some eggs. I hope you all enjoy!

Ingredients & Kitchen Utensils Needed:

  • Bag of Ori-Eda hash browns
  • Uncured smoked ham
  • American cheese slices
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Frying pan
  • 12-inch skillet
  • Spatula
  • Serrated knife
  • Cutting board

Cooking Steps

  1. Cut the ham into chunks using a serrated knife and cutting board
  2. Place the 12-inch skillet on a stove eye that is set to medium-high heat
  3. Coat the inside of the skillet with 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  4. Empty half of the Ori-Eda's hash brown bag as an even layer in the prepared skillet
  5. Sprinkle some salt and black pepper onto the hash browns for flavoring
  6. Allow the hash browns to cook for 5-7 minutes
  7. Place the frying pan on a different stove eye that is set to medium heat
  8. Coat the inside of the frying pan with butter
  9. Cook the chunked ham in the frying pan until it is tender
  10. While the ham is cooking, sprinkle some salt and black pepper for flavoring
  11. Once the hash browns have cooked for 5-7 minutes, flip them and cook for an additional 2-4 minutes
  12. After the hash browns and chunked ham are cooked, add the ham into the skillet and lower heat
  13. Place slices of American cheese on top of the hash browns and ham
